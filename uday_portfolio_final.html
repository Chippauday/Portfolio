01
NLP · Content Moderation

Toxic Comment Classification

A production-grade NLP system that detects hate speech, cyberbullying, threats, and offensive language — replacing slow manual review with intelligent, real-time automation — achieving 95% classification accuracy.

PythonNLPTF-IDFNaive Bayesscikit-learnPandasWord Embeddings
Objective
Enable automated real-time content moderation at social platform scale, reducing reliance on slow manual review teams.
Approach
Full text preprocessing pipeline — tokenisation, stop-word removal, lemmatisation — combined with TF-IDF and word embeddings feeding a tuned Naive Bayes classifier.
Outcome
95% classification accuracy on large labeled dataset. Significantly lower false-positive rate vs keyword-only filters. Ready as a plug-in moderation layer for live platforms.
Impact
Demonstrates how contextual NLP outperforms rule-based approaches — laying groundwork for future transformer-based upgrades.
02
Computer Vision · Public Safety

Real-Time Accident Detection System

A CNN-based pipeline that monitors dashcams and traffic cameras live, detects accidents in real time, and dispatches automatic emergency alerts — achieving an estimated ~60% reduction in response time.

PythonCNNKerasTensorFlowOpenCVNumPy
Peer-Reviewed · Published in IJIRSET
Objective
Automate accident detection across city-scale surveillance, drastically cutting emergency response times without requiring human operators in the loop.
Approach
CNN pipeline with frame-level temporal feature extraction. Custom logic for collision events, abnormal braking, and irregular vehicle motion using OpenCV spatial analysis.
Outcome
Achieved ~60% reduction in estimated emergency response time. System accurately identifies accident-prone events from live feeds and dispatches alerts without human operators in the loop.
Recognition
Findings peer-reviewed and published in IJIRSET. Validated for deployment in smart traffic management and city-level safety infrastructure.
